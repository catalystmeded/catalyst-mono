About Catalyst

Advancing patient care through cost-effective, outcomes-driven education.

Catalyst Medical Education, LLC is an independent medical education company that designs and delivers accredited continuing education programs for healthcare professionals. Our funding comes entirely through the regulated medical education grant process — commercial supporters in pharma and biotech fund programs that close identified clinical practice gaps.

We operate as a lean, expert-led team. This means our clients work directly with senior, credentialed professionals at every stage — not account managers. Our mission is to speed the translation of the latest clinical data into everyday practice, and every program we deliver is designed to prove it.

Our Approach

A planning methodology built for every phase of drug development.

Our PBLI-based framework aligns educational strategy with where a therapy is in its lifecycle — from pre-approval needs assessment through post-launch performance optimization — ensuring education is always timely, targeted, and evidence-grounded.

1

Establishing Needs

Before any program is designed, we identify and document the gaps.

  • Literature review & expert opinion
  • Learner surveys & EMR data
  • Change Readiness Inventory
  • Pilot programs
2

Knowledge Gaps

Expert-driven methods to build foundational awareness and understanding.

  • Evidence-based symposia
  • Disease/state awareness
  • MoA education
  • Traditional & digital media
3

Competence Gaps

Learner-driven formats that translate knowledge into clinical skill.

  • Case-based discussion
  • Shared decision-making
  • Team-based care models
  • PBLI Stage A & B
4

Performance Gaps

System-level methods that embed change into clinical practice.

  • Quality Improvement
  • Barrier mitigation
  • PBLI Stage C
  • Outcomes measurement

What We Do

Built for every stage of the education lifecycle.

From needs assessment through outcomes reporting, Catalyst manages the full continuum of independent medical education — live, virtual, and enduring, domestic and global.

Curriculum & Instructional Design

Every program begins with a rigorous needs assessment and a clearly defined instructional framework grounded in adult learning principles — ensuring activities close specific, documented gaps in clinical practice.

Live & Virtual Symposia

Full-service planning and execution of live, virtual, and hybrid educational events — symposia, roundtables, dinner programs, satellite sessions — with expert faculty and engagement tools built for HCP audiences at major medical meetings worldwide.

Learning Centers & Serial Education

On-demand modules, webcasts, and enduring learning experiences structured for ongoing engagement. We build and host dedicated learning center websites with reinforcing resources — infographics, FAQ tools, guidelines access, and learner alerts.

MoA Animations

Scientifically accurate mechanism-of-action animations that clarify complex therapeutic concepts, improve retention, and elevate the learner experience across live and enduring formats. Optimized for conference display and on-demand delivery.

Global Education & Translations

International program development with ISO 17100–certified translations in 5+ languages — not just language conversion, but clinical, cultural, and regulatory adaptation for each market. AI is never used for direct translation.

Outcomes+ Reporting & Insights

Our enhanced outcomes framework delivers cohort-level statistical analyses, predictive modeling, and financial impact estimates — giving supporters the data they need and the intelligence to plan future education strategically.

Patient Interviews & Education

Patient and care-partner voice integrated into CE — video interviews, patient learning portals, and caregiver-focused infographics — supporting the ACCME's emphasis on patient perspectives in accredited education.

Pharmacy & Allied Health Education

Accredited CE for pharmacists, nurses, and allied health professionals, developed through our strategic partnership with the University of Tennessee College of Pharmacy and distributed via PowerPak and other specialty channels.

Needs Assessments

Rigorous, publication-quality gap analyses grounded in literature review, expert opinion, learner surveys, and real-world data — building the scientific rationale for every grant submission and educational initiative.
