About Catalyst

Dedicated to improving care through education

Catalyst Medical Education is an independent medical education company founded on the belief that well-designed, scientifically rigorous education is one of the most powerful levers available to improve healthcare outcomes.

Our team brings together decades of expertise in instructional design, outcomes methodology, clinical content development, and program management — working across live and enduring formats, domestically and globally.

What We Do

Educational programs that move the needle

From live symposia to global digital enduring activities, every program we build creates measurable change in clinical practice.

Live Educational Activities

Symposia, roundtables, dinner programs, and conference satellite sessions with expert faculty and audience engagement tools designed for maximum knowledge transfer.

Enduring Online Education

On-demand modules, webcasts, and multimedia learning experiences delivered through our accredited provider partnerships — available when and where clinicians need them.

MoA Animations & Rich Media

Scientifically accurate mechanism-of-action animations, patient perspectives, and multimedia assets that clarify complex concepts and drive measurable improvements in retention.

Global Education

International program development, translation services, and culturally adapted content for HCP audiences outside the US across multiple therapeutic areas.

Outcomes Measurement

Validated assessment tools, pre/post analyses, and comprehensive outcomes reporting that demonstrates educational impact and identifies continuing gaps in practice.

Needs Assessments

Rigorous gap analyses grounded in literature review, claims data, expert advisory input, and learner feedback — building the scientific rationale for every educational initiative.
