Our Approach

A planning methodology built for every phase of drug development

Our PBLI-based framework aligns educational strategy with where a therapy sits in its lifecycle — from pre-approval needs assessment through post-launch performance optimization.

01. Establishing Needs

Literature review, expert opinion, learner surveys, EMR data, and Change Readiness Inventory — building a defensible gap analysis and scientific rationale before a single learning objective is written.

02. Knowledge Gaps — Expert-Driven Methods

Symposia, evidence-based discussion, MoA education, and disease state awareness — designed for specialists and primary care audiences at NDA and launch phases.

03. Competence Gaps — Learner-Driven Methods

Case-based discussions, shared decision-making, team-based care, and PBLI Stage A & B — moving learners from awareness to demonstrated clinical skill.

04. Performance Gaps — System-Driven Methods

Quality improvement, barrier mitigation, and PBLI Stage C — embedding change into clinical practice and measuring outcomes at the performance level.

What We Do

Built for every stage of the education lifecycle

From curriculum design through outcomes reporting, Catalyst manages the full continuum of independent medical education — live, virtual, and enduring.

Curriculum & Instructional Design

Every program begins with a rigorous needs assessment and a clearly defined instructional framework — ensuring educational activities are grounded in evidence and designed to close specific gaps in clinical practice.

Live & Virtual Symposia

Full-service planning and execution of live and virtual educational events — symposia, roundtables, dinner programs, and conference satellite sessions — with expert faculty and engagement tools built for HCP audiences.

Learning Centers & Serial Education

On-demand modules, webcasts, and enduring learning experiences delivered through our accredited provider partnerships — structured for ongoing learner engagement across a disease area or therapeutic series.

MoA Animations

Scientifically accurate mechanism-of-action animations that clarify complex therapeutic concepts, improve knowledge retention, and elevate the overall learner experience across both live and enduring formats.

Global Education & Translations

International program development and culturally adapted content for HCP audiences outside the US — not just language translation, but clinical, cultural, and regulatory adaptation for each market.

Outcomes+ Reporting & Insights

Our proprietary outcomes framework delivers validated pre/post analyses, practice-change data, and actionable reporting — giving supporters the evidence they need and informing opportunities for future education.

Patient Interviews

Compelling patient perspectives integrated into educational content to humanize therapeutic concepts, build empathy, and contextualize clinical decision-making within the real patient journey.
